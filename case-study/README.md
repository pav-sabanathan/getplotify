# Case Study

This folder contains the end-to-end case study for Plotify — 
documenting the full journey from problem identification through 
to a live, deployed product.

## What the Case Study Covers

- The personal frustration that became the product idea
- Who the user is and why I used myself as the primary user at MVP
- The research and discovery process before any building began
- Every significant product decision made and the reasoning behind it
- How the product was built using Lovable and what that process looked like
- What I'd build next and what I learned along the way
- A retrospective look at the original success metrics vs the outcome

---

## Why This Exists

Most PM portfolios show the output — a slide deck, a wireframe, 
a list of features shipped. This case study shows the thinking 
behind the output. Every decision documented here was made before, 
during, or immediately after building — not retrofitted afterwards.

That distinction matters. Anyone can describe what they built. 
This documents why.
